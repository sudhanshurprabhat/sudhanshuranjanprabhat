About

I design and coordinate water supply, sewerage, irrigation, and firefighting networks for major developments — the kind of infrastructure that only gets noticed when it fails. Most of my work happens in the gap between hydraulic theory and what an authority will actually approve.

This site is where I write that gap down: modelling workflows, NOC submission lessons, coefficient tables, and the occasional argument for why a spec should be questioned rather than copied. Edit this paragraph freely — it's a placeholder for your own voice.
